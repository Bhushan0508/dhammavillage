What is Dhamma Village? Dhamma Village is a structured residential environment designed for disciplined meditation practice, ethical living, and sustainable community operations. It is not a retreat center or tourism project.Q2. Is Dhamma Village affiliated with any religious organization? No. Dhamma Village operates as a non-profit trust focused on meditation-based discipline and community structure. It does not promote sectarian worship.Q3. Is this a commercial project? No. All operations are non-profit. Revenue from resources or programs is used only for operational sustainability and infrastructure development.PARTICIPATIONQ4. Can anyone join the village? Only individuals willing to follow fixed schedules, ethical conduct rules, and community discipline protocols are accepted. Casual participation is not allowed.Q5. Are short visits permitted? No walk-in visits. All visits require prior approval and defined purpose.Q6. Is prior meditation experience required? Yes. Participants must have basic familiarity with silent sitting practice. This is not a beginner training center.ACTIVITIESQ7. Are daily activities mandatory? Yes for residents. Optional for remote participants. Attendance is logged.Q8. Are phones and internet allowed? Restricted usage only in designated zones. No devices in meditation or silence areas.VOLUNTEERINGQ9. Can I volunteer for a few days? No. Minimum commitment periods apply. Casual volunteering is rejected.Q10. Are volunteers paid? No. Volunteers receive accommodation and meals. No monetary compensation.DONATIONSQ11. Are donations tax-deductible? Yes. Contributions qualify under 80G for Indian donors. Foreign donations follow FCRA compliance.Q12. How are donations used? Only for infrastructure, operations, and replication projects. Financial reports are published annually.RESOURCESQ13. Can I purchase resources without joining the village? Yes for general items. Restricted materials require registration.GOVERNANCEQ14. Who manages Dhamma Village? A registered Trust governed by named trustees. Annual audits ensure compliance.DATA &amp; PRIVACYQ15. Is my personal data shared? No. Data is used only for internal operations and aggregated analytics. No commercial sharing.CONTACTQ16. How can I reach the organization?
