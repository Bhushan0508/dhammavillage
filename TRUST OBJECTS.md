# **TRUST OBJECTS**

*(Primary & Ancillary -- Maharashtra Public Charitable Trust)*

## **PRIMARY OBJECTS**

1.  **To promote mental well-being, emotional stability, and inner
    discipline** among individuals and communities through non-clinical,
    preventive, and community-based methods.

2.  **To establish, develop, and operate community-based residential and
    non-residential facilities** aimed at preventive mental health,
    ethical living, and social harmony, particularly in rural and
    underprivileged areas.

3.  **To undertake rural development initiatives** that integrate mental
    well-being, disciplined living, and community participation with
    daily livelihood activities.

4.  **To design and implement preventive mental health programs** that
    focus on emotional regulation, stress reduction, behavioral
    stability, and ethical conduct without medical or religious
    intervention.

5.  **To work for the upliftment of underprivileged and marginalized
    communities** by providing access to stable living environments,
    disciplined routines, and supportive community structures.

## **ANCILLARY / SUPPORTING OBJECTS**

6.  To establish and maintain simple residential units, community halls,
    sanitation facilities, kitchens, and other basic infrastructure
    required for community living and well-being initiatives.

7.  To promote sustainable living practices including agriculture, water
    conservation, renewable energy, sanitation, and environmentally
    responsible infrastructure.

8.  To organize educational, awareness, and training activities related
    to emotional well-being, ethical conduct, self-discipline, and
    community responsibility.

9.  To collaborate with government departments, local authorities,
    public institutions, and non-governmental organizations for
    implementation, convergence, and scaling of social development
    programs.

10. To receive grants, donations, CSR contributions, aid, and assistance
    from government bodies, public sector undertakings, private
    organizations, trusts, institutions, and individuals, both in India
    and abroad, subject to applicable laws.

11. To acquire, hold, lease, develop, and maintain movable and immovable
    property solely for the objectives of the Trust and not for personal
    or commercial benefit.

12. To conduct research, documentation, monitoring, and evaluation of
    community-based mental well-being and rural development models for
    learning and replication.

## **NON-PROFIT & COMPLIANCE CLAUSES (MANDATORY)**

13. The Trust shall operate **strictly on a non-profit basis**, and no
    portion of its income or property shall be distributed directly or
    indirectly to trustees or members.

14. The activities of the Trust shall be **non-religious, non-political,
    and non-commercial** in nature and shall be open to all persons
    without discrimination.

15. The Trust shall utilize its income and assets solely for the
    promotion of its objects.

16. Upon dissolution of the Trust, the remaining assets shall be
    transferred to another trust or institution having similar
    charitable objects and enjoying registration under applicable laws.
