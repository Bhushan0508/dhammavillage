## **WEB DEVELOPER HANDOFF PACK**

## **1. PROJECT OVERVIEW (FOR DEVELOPER)**

**Project Type:** Informational + credibility website\
**Purpose:**

-   Government & CSR credibility

-   Clear explanation of project model

-   Partnership and contact intake

**NOT a fundraising-heavy site.\
** **NOT a spiritual/religious site.**

Primary audience:

-   Government officers

-   CSR heads

-   Foundations

-   Serious collaborators

## **2. TECH STACK (RECOMMENDED)**

### **Option A (Preferred -- Simple & Stable)**

-   **WordPress\
    **

-   Clean theme (no animations)

-   Gutenberg or Elementor (basic use only)

### **Option B (If custom)**

-   HTML5 + CSS3

-   Minimal JS

-   No heavy frameworks

**Do NOT use:\
** ❌ Fancy animations\
❌ Parallax scrolling\
❌ Video-heavy landing pages

Government officers use slow systems.

-   **Domain:** dhamma-village.org (or approved variant)

-   **Email:\

    -   contact@domain

**Sections (in order)**
